About

The aim of UK Pray is for every student at the University of Kentucky to have salvation that can only come through knowing Christ.

In order to pray for 30,159 names in the year 2021 we are asking for 754 people to pray for 40 names for 40 days.

This will take place starting February 17th and go until Easter Sunday, April 4th.

Why?

We believe that God's heart is for us to join Him in praying for His people (Hebrews 7:25, Romans 8:34, 1 John 2:6)

Because we live in Lexington, we believe God has called us to pray specifically for the individual people in Lexington. (Jerimiah 29:7, Galatians 5:14)

We believe that God can make the University of Kentucky a light for the nations, and we are asking Him to do it! (Matthew 5:14, Psalms 2:8)

We live for a moment in this world (James 4:14) and are called to be lights to the world (Isaiah 49:6). Please join us in prayer for our city's campus as we ask God to do something only He can do. (Ephesians 3:20, 2 Peter 3:9)
